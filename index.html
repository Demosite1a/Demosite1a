Ayappakkam is the capital Village of Ambattur Town. It is the most populous Village in the Ayappakkam village, with a metropolitan area of over Ambattur.

Standing on the Ayappakkam Lake, Ayappakkam has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Ayappakians, who named it as Ayappakkam.
